Description

Caffeine makes any iPhone app faster than Amazon's, while improving its security. People frequently call us "the real world Pied Piper" (though, their compression-based approach actually introduces a security flaw).

Any developer can now achieve as much as PhDs at Amazon by dragging and dropping the Caffeine library!

  • Sending an image to Austin, TX from DC using Caffeine is faster than sending the same image to Austin, TX from Dallas without Caffeine (link)
  • Caffeine has saved over 3 months of time across hundreds of thousands of users in dozens of apps

History

Co-founder Drew Crawford published the famous blog post Why Mobile Web Apps Are Slow, which outlined the problematic thinking endemic to treating native mobile apps as web apps. This kickstarted our exploration into building native-app technology from the ground up (without the bulk of the web). More on the about page.

It is known that conventional network security (HTTPS) slows the speed of the network. During our work with Apple, Microsoft, and other clients, we noticed that they will typically choose to sacrifice network security for app speed.

Caffeine deletes the dichotomy between speed and security.
