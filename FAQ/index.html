Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does your network cover?

Our network is nation-wide! We go over Sprint and T-mobile towers. So to check a specific area for coverage click HERE or give us a call at 414-939-8586!


Will you lock my phone?

No! We never lock phones nor do we lock phone numbers. You are always able to move to another carrier in an instant.


Why have I never heard of you?

We are a fairly new cell phone service that is growing everyday! One of the benefits of joining a smaller network like us is you get premium service and our complete attention! We started Wallace Murry not to be the biggest compnay, but to have the best customer expereince.


What happens if I want travel internationally?

Almost half of your users plan on traveling internationally this year! Before you leave the country, just give us a call and we will supply you with a temporary international SIM card to be used while you are outside the U.S. You only have to pay about $10 for the SIM card and we will take care of the rest!


Will I get to keep my phone, phone number, and contacts?

You will ALWAYS be able to keep your phone number! However, your current phone may not be compatible with our network. Just give us a call at 414-939-8586 to find out if your phone will work! If you do need to get a new device we give you a set of options that are best suited to your needs - we never push expensive products on you! We will then bring the new device to you and transfer all of your contact, pictures, videos, and apps over to the new device free of charge!


How many customers do you have?

As of 5/7/2018 we have about 100 customers and are growing everyday! We have numerous customers that will happily provide references as well!


What happens if Wallace Murry Phone Company dissolves?

Although we do not see this happening any time soon, we will always proctect your interests! Wallace Murry has provisions in place so that if all of the employees were to suddenly die or the company were dissolved all customers would be immediately warned and seemlessly moved over to the Sprint network. You will NEVER lose your phone number or service!
